Personal Project · 个人项目
CreatorHub

An AI-powered cross-platform content growth assistant for global creators — automatically adapting a single piece of content to fit the native culture and format of each platform.

面向全球创作者的 AI 跨平台内容增长助手，将单一内容自动适配至不同平台，实现从"内容翻译"向"内容平台化改写"的升级。

2025 — 2026
Product Design AI / LLM Creator Economy
01

Insight — Where the Problem Comes From 洞察 — 问题从哪里来

Creators going global face a core contradiction: the same content consistently underperforms across platforms because of differences in platform culture, user behavior, content structure, and engagement mechanics. Translation alone cannot solve cross-platform distribution — this is a "platformization" problem, not a language problem.

内容创作者在出海过程中面临一个核心矛盾：同一篇内容在不同平台往往因平台文化、用户偏好、内容结构及互动机制差异而表现不佳。简单翻译无法解决跨平台传播问题，这是一个"平台化"问题，而非"语言"问题。

02

Solution — Platform Adaptation Engine 解法 — 平台适配引擎

Designed a Platform Adaptation Engine that automatically analyzes content topic, target audience, and platform characteristics — intelligently reconstructing titles, body structure, tone, tag strategy, and CTAs to generate a version that fits each platform's content ecosystem.

设计平台适配引擎（Platform Adaptation Engine），自动分析内容主题、目标受众及平台特征，对标题、正文结构、表达风格、标签策略、互动引导（CTA）等进行智能重构，生成符合目标平台内容生态的版本。

Work Project · 工作项目 · BitMart
AI Customer Service — $90K Problem, Built in 3 Weeks

Designed and delivered BitMart's AI-first customer support system from scratch — replacing $90K/year in tooling costs, covering 13 languages globally, and establishing a scalable framework for multilingual support automation.

从零设计并交付 BitMart 智能客服系统，替代年费 $90K 的客服工具，覆盖全球 13 种语言，建立可扩展的多语言客服自动化框架。

BitMart · 2024
AI / LLM Claude Code Prototype 13 Languages Cost Reduction
01

Context — $90K/Year, <5% Resolution 背景 — 年花 $90K，解决率不足 5%

BitMart was spending nearly $90,000 annually on customer support tooling, but AI was resolving less than 5% of user inquiries. Most conversations still required human agents, while the support operation served users across 13 languages globally. I was tasked with building an AI-powered customer support experience from scratch.

BitMart 每年在客服工具上投入近 $90,000，但 AI 解决率不足 5%——绝大多数对话仍需人工介入，且需覆盖全球 13 种语言的用户。我被委以重任，从零搭建一套 AI 驱动的客服体验。

02

Approach — Tickets First, Then Build 方法论 — 先读懂工单，再动手建系统

Before designing anything, I analyzed thousands of historical support tickets and mapped the most frequent issues into structured intent categories. I then designed the end-to-end customer support workflow — and instead of using Figma, I built an interactive prototype directly with Claude Code, so stakeholders could experience the full customer journey rather than just view static screens.

在设计任何方案之前，我先分析了大量历史工单，将高频问题归类为结构化意图类别。随后设计了端到端的客服工作流——但我没有用 Figma，而是直接用 Claude Code 构建了一个可交互的原型，让所有相关方都能真实体验完整的用户旅程，而不只是浏览静态页面。
